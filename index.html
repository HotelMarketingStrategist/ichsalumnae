Club & Beach Access

Beach days, resort-style touches, and easy island moments.

The Tryall Club

Guests of Windsong and Bambu enjoy access to The Tryall Club, a private club with complimentary amenities such as the beach, non-motorized watersports, tennis courts, fitness center, jogging trails, Hummingbird Kids Club, Great House afternoon tea, the Manager’s Cocktail Party, select special events, the Tryall pool, and the Usain Bolt Games Room.

Golf, spa services, dining, bar purchases, motorized watersports, tennis shop purchases, ball boys, and certain club events or activities are available at an additional cost and subject to availability. Temporary membership dues are paid directly to Tryall and are currently US$45 per day for guests aged 16 and over, US$25 per day for guests aged 13–16, and complimentary for children aged 12 and under.

Half Moon Beach Club

Spring Farm villas include access to Half Moon Beach Club, with complimentary resort membership benefits such as Eclipse Beach, jacuzzi, tennis courts, fitness center, croquet, and miniature putting green. Spa, horseback riding, watersports, and tennis services may be booked on the day of service and are subject to availability and fees. No food or beverages are allowed on the Half Moon property, towel service is not included, and admittance is limited to 10 guests per villa.
